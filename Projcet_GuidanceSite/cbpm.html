温州肯恩大学校园建筑设计寻求多样性的变化，为校园的创新设计提供了极大的空间。校园不强求统一性的协调，而鼓励建筑在多样性中沟通、对话，寻找平衡协调的途径与方法。因此，不同于校园内其他建筑，商学院以现代化的设计风格和明快的色彩搭配，为温州肯恩大学校园平添了几分个性和活力。同时，商学院的立面选色和大台阶等设计元素，与校园现有建筑相互呼应，在多元化发展中形成沟通和对话。建筑各层设计宽窄各异的室外平台，在不同方向上相连接，从而形成了一条环绕建筑的连续步道，为学生提供了风景优美的室外学习活动场所，同时，水平外廊还起到夏日遮阳的作用。商学院室内空间适应美国启发式、灵活性、多样性教育方式的要求，家具、智能化教学设备力求舒适、美观、先进。同时满足在职培训、国际学术交流的需求。

The architectural design of the Wenzhou Cane University campus seeks diversity and variation, providing great scope for innovative design of the campus. Rather than imposing uniformity and coordination, the campus encourages buildings to communicate and dialogue in diversity, finding ways and means to balance and harmonize. Therefore, unlike other buildings on campus, the School of Business adds a bit of individuality and vitality to the Wenzhou Cane University campus with its modern design style and bright color palette. At the same time, the design elements such as the color selection of the facade and the large steps of the business school echo the existing buildings on campus, forming communication and dialogue in the diversified development. Outdoor terraces of varying widths are designed on each level of the building, connecting in different directions to create a continuous walkway around the building, providing students with a scenic outdoor learning space, while the horizontal exterior corridors provide shade in the summer. The interior space of the business school is adapted to the requirements of the American inspirational, flexible and diverse educational approach, and the furniture and intelligent teaching equipment are designed to be comfortable, beautiful and advanced. It also meets the needs of on-the-job training and international academic exchanges.
