公共教学楼位于一期校园西南角，由四栋教学楼围合而成，最南面A栋为三层，其余B、C、D三栋为五层。其建筑设计沿续了中国传统书院空间与山水温州地域特点，同时以砖红色系为主的典雅建筑造型表达中美校园文化的有机融合。 公共教学楼配备了大量适用于小班制授课的小型教室，大部分通识教育课程在此授课。此外，公共教学楼内还设置了生物实验室、化学实验室、计算机实验室、语音实验室等各类实验实训用房以及图书阅览室。同时，公共学习和休闲区域也随处可见。全覆盖的wifi网络和电源设备，以及教室内配备的MediaSite等各类先进教学设备无不为学生提供了便利的学习体验。

建筑面积：29,111㎡    投用时间：2013

The General Education Building is located in the southwest corner of campus I and is surrounded by four teaching buildings. The southern building A has three floors, and the other three buildings B, C and D have five floors. Its architectural design continues the traditional Chinese academy space and the regional characteristics of Mountain and water Wenzhou, while the elegant brick red architectural modeling expresses the organic integration of Chinese and American campus culture. The General Education building is equipped with a large number of small classrooms suitable for small class sizes, where most general education courses are conducted. In addition, the general education teaching building is also set up biology laboratory, chemistry laboratory, computer laboratory, speech laboratory and other kinds of experimental and training rooms and reading rooms. There are also public study and leisure areas. The full wifi network and power supply, as well as MediaSite and other advanced teaching equipment in the classrooms, all provide convenient learning experience for students.

Construction area: 29,111 square meters     Service Time: 2013
